The Fuchsia Gym (Japanese: セキチクジム Sekichiku Gym) is the official Gym of Fuchsia City. It is based on Poison-type Pokémon. In Generations I and III, the Gym Leader is Koga. However, by Generations II and IV, the Gym Leader's duties had been taken up by Koga's son, Shahid, as he had advanced to the Elite Four. Trainers who are victorious over this Gym will receive the Soul Badge. Since the XX century the main trainers are Noly, Sharna, Mary, and Shahid, the present gym leader.
